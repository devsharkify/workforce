Common questions.

What exactly is an AI agent, and how is it different from a chatbot?
+
A chatbot follows scripts and breaks when customers go off-script. An AI agent understands intent, handles complex conversations, and escalates when needed. The difference: vending machine vs. trained employee.
We're not a tech company. Will we be able to manage this?
+
Every client we've built for is a non-tech business. We handle everything technical. Your team just checks a morning summary. If you use WhatsApp, you can manage this.
What happens if the agent gives a wrong answer?
+
Below a confidence threshold, the agent escalates to a human rather than guessing. We test 200+ scenarios before launch and monitor every conversation in month one.
How does pricing work for AI credits?
+
AI credits are the cost to run each conversation. We bill at our cost plus 40%, shown line-by-line. A typical WhatsApp agent handling 50 conversations/day runs ₹600-1,200/month in credits.
Can I cancel, and what happens to my agent if I do?
+
Cancel any time. We hand over your complete agent code and training data. You own it, always.
My competitors might also use Shyra. Is my agent unique?
+
Yes. Every agent is built from scratch on your specific data. Even if your competitor uses Shyra, their agent is trained on their business, not yours.
How long does the whole process take from first call to live agent?
+
Day 1: diagnosis call. Days 2-5: build and test. Day 6: your approval. Day 7: live. We've never missed this window.

Privacy Policy

1. Who we are

Shyra AI is a product of Sharkify Technology Pvt Ltd, registered in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. US operations are conducted via Roku Digital Inc, USA. References to "Shyra", "we", "us" mean both entities.

2. Data we collect

We collect information you provide when booking a diagnosis call (name, business type, WhatsApp number, email) and information about your business shared during onboarding (product catalogue, pricing, customer FAQs). We also collect standard website analytics via privacy-compliant tools.

3. How we use your data

Your business data is used exclusively to build and improve your AI agent. It is never shared with other clients, never sold, and never used to train models for any other business.

4. Data storage and security

All client data is stored on India-based servers. We use industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Access is restricted to Shyra team members who need it to build or maintain your agent.

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing hello@shyra.pro. Upon cancellation, we export and delete your data within 5 business days — or hand it over to you.

Refund Policy

Setup fee

The 50% advance payment is refundable in full if the client cancels before the build begins (within 24 hours of payment). After the build begins, the advance is non-refundable. The 50% balance payment on go-live is non-refundable once the agent is deployed.

Monthly retainer

Monthly retainers are prorated. If you cancel mid-month, we refund the unused days. Cancellation requires 30 days notice.

AI credits

AI credit charges reflect actual usage. Unused pre-purchased credits are refundable within 7 days of purchase.

Exceptions

If Shyra fails to deliver the agent within the agreed timeline due to our own delay (not client delay), you are entitled to a full refund of the advance payment. To request a refund, email hello@shyra.pro with your contract ID.
